QUESTIONS FROM YOUR COLLEAGUES

What makes 4Scotty different than all other job portals?

At 4Scotty, the focus is on you. no time-consuming job applications – just lean back, relax and let top companies come to you with individual job offers (including salary details!). Mass mailings? No way! The entire process is in your hands and your profile data is 100% secure. Plus, if you find a job then you can look forward to a € 1,101.10 starting bonus.

Do I need to apply for the job?

We designed the processes as simple and intuitive as possible. There is no need to wade through tons of unsuitable job listings. Just let top companies compete for you with individual IT job offers (including salary details!). Our filters automatically block spam.

Who can benefit from 4Scotty?

Anyone working in the IT sector as an employee: software developers, product and project managers, engineering managers, quality assurance experts, UX/UI designers, system administrators, data scientists, technical consultants and sales support engineers. Whether you're actively looking for a new job or just want to look around, at 4Scotty you take it the easy way.

Is it free to join 4Scotty?

Yes! As an IT expert, you do not have to pay to join. If you get an interview via 4Scotty, the interested company will cover your travel costs. When you find a new job we will even share our commission with you.

How do I sign up?

It just takes a few clicks to create your profile at 4Scotty. We will contact you shortly thereafter and activate your account. You can then begin receiving individual job offers that are custom tailored to your wishes and qualifications.

I’m not really looking for a new job. Is 4Scotty still right for me?

Absolutely! Many IT experts don’t realize how much their experience is worth. The salary information in our no obligation job offers can help you gauge your market value and offer a great way to keep an eye open for exciting new projects. Your current employer cannot see your 4Scotty profile.
