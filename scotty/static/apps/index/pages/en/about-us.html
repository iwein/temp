Welcome to a new world of recruitinG

Today’s IT recruiting process is a real challenge – for both sides.

Employers desperately seek IT professionals and often go to great lengths but with poor results. If a company’s IT department does not have the right experts, this can lead to problems for their business. But the fact remains that suitable candidates are just hard to find.

On the other hand software experts are aware of the talent they have but are not sure where they can put their skills to better use other than their current job. They face a confusing job market. Job offers look the same and the information they offer, concerning IT systems in use and salary details are not shared openly. Unsolicited job offers from headhunters are often unacceptable.

We created 4Scotty to fix the core of this process. We are convinced that openness during job placements is where everyone can profit by putting their cards on the table. The key to

Our success is based on fulfilling the job requirements and desires of IT experts. Unlike other platforms, our candidates don’t apply to companies, but companies compete for candidates with specific job offers. The candidate can then decides which offer is attractive enough to justify a personal meeting.

Chief engineer of the USS Enterprise on “Star Trek”, Montgomery Scott, better known as Scotty, is known as a technological genius.
